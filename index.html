Top Destinations

Discover the beauty of Nepal's seven provinces, each offering unique landscapes, cultures, and adventures.

Everest Base Camp

Everest Base Camp is a dream destination for adventure seekers, offering breathtaking views of the world’s highest peak, Mount Everest. Located in the Himalayas, this legendary trekking route takes you through picturesque Sherpa villages, ancient monasteries, and rugged mountain landscapes. Trekkers experience the thrill of high-altitude hiking while witnessing the Khumbu Glacier and Everest’s towering beauty up close. Whether you seek adventure or a spiritual journey, Everest Base Camp promises an unforgettable experience in the heart of the Himalayas.

Koshi Tappu Wildlife Reserve

Koshi Tappu Wildlife Reserve is a paradise for nature lovers and bird watchers, offering a unique blend of rich biodiversity and scenic beauty. Located in the eastern Terai region of Nepal, this wetland sanctuary is home to over 500 species of birds, including rare migratory species, making it a dream destination for bird enthusiasts. Visitors can also spot endangered wildlife such as the wild water buffalo (Arna) and Gangetic dolphins while exploring the lush grasslands and tranquil Koshi River. Whether you're seeking an adventurous jungle safari or a peaceful retreat into nature, Koshi Tappu promises an unforgettable experience amidst Nepal’s stunning wilderness.

Pathivara Temple

Pathivara Temple is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site located in the Taplejung district of eastern Nepal. Perched at an altitude of 3,794 meters, it is dedicated to Goddess Pathivara, believed to fulfill the wishes of devotees. The temple attracts thousands of pilgrims, especially from Nepal and India, seeking blessings for prosperity and protection. The journey to Pathivara involves a scenic trek through lush rhododendron forests, offering breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range. Whether for religious devotion or adventure, Pathivara Temple is a serene and spiritually uplifting destination.
